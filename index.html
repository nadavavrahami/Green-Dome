About Us

The GreenDome initiative, provides a sustainable oasis of shade and clean air, where ever the sun shines.

With temperatures rising every year, even day to day earns such as waiting for a bus, have gotten harder and hotter. A GreenDomeâ€™s sleek innovative design, combined with a sustainable energy solution, guarantees that the best part of a bus ride, will be waiting for it to arrive. The standalone dome, absorbs the suns energy and immediately powers a top of the line fan, the air is then pushed by the fan through a high efficiency particulate air filter (HEPA), ensuring that even the hottest days at a bus stop feel like an autumn breeze by the sea.

Our Principals

Innovative

Revolutionizing the way the world waits. Finding a solution that is good for us and the planet.

Sustainable

Harnessing the suns energy. Every GreenDome is an everlasting sanctuary of clean air.

Sleek Street Art

The standalone dome can be fitted into a wide variety of installations, allowing each GreenDome to be a unique work of art.
